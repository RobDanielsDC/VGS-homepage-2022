Welcome to
Video Game Sage

A community of fans who celebrate video games old and new. Whether you’re a gamer, collector, creator, or seller, you can find a home here.

Meet Characters of all Kinds

Everything we do falls back on the four pillars of our community. Join in on some friendly competition in our weekly NES contests, show off your favourite new collectors piece, discuss the semantics around coding a new game, or just sell some old unneeded items. Whatever areas of the site you want to participate in, you are more than welcome!

GAMERS

The Knight

A noble gamer, competitive and dexterous. Always eager to face new challenges, a new quest, or a new personal best.

COLLECTORS

The Dragon

Adamantly on guard, the fierce collector keeps a close eye on their stash of games. Keeping them orderly and preserved.

HOMEBREWERS

The Alchemist

Creative and resourceful. A homebrewer burns the midnight oil, using their skills to craft new experiences to share with the community.

SELLERS

The Merchant

The thrifty seller acquires rare gems to add to their stock. They strike deals with their trusted customers and help supply the community.
